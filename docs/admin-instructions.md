## Adding a New Product

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Open the Glide app** on your phone

2. **Tap the "+" button** (usually at bottom or top right)

3. **Fill in product details**:
   - **Product ID**: Enter a unique code (e.g., `prod016`)
     - Tip: Use `prod` + number (prod001, prod002, etc.)
   
   - **Category**: Choose from dropdown
     - Options: Vegetables, Fruits, Dairy, Bakery, Snacks
   
   - **Product Name**: Enter the product name
     - Example: "Fresh Tomatoes"
   
   - **Price**: Enter price in Rupees (numbers only)
     - Example: `45` (for ‚Çπ45)
   
   - **Unit**: Choose from dropdown
     - Options: kg (kilogram), ltr (liter), pc (piece), dozen
   
   - **Photo**: Tap to add product image
     - **From Camera**: Take a new photo
     - **From Gallery**: Choose existing photo
     - Tip: Use clear, well-lit photos showing the product
   
   - **Description**: Write a short description
     - Example: "Juicy red tomatoes, locally sourced"
   
   - **Stock**: Enter how many units you have
     - Example: `30` (for 30 kg/liters/pieces available)
   
   - **Visible**: Turn ON (green switch)
     - ON = Customers can see this product
     - OFF = Product is hidden from customers
   
4. **Tap "Save" or "Submit"**

5. **Wait 30-60 seconds** for the product to appear in the customer app

## Editing a Product

### To update price, stock, or other details:

1. **Open the Glide app**

2. **Find the product** you want to edit
   - Scroll through the list or use search

3. **Tap on the product** to open details

4. **Tap "Edit" button** (pencil icon or Edit button)

5. **Change any information** you need:
   - Common edits: Price, Stock, Description
   - You can also change the photo

6. **Tap "Save"**

7. **Changes appear in customer app** within 1 minute

---

## Hiding a Product

### To temporarily remove a product from customer view:

1. **Open the product** (tap on it)

2. **Tap "Edit"**

3. **Find the "Visible" switch**

4. **Turn it OFF** (it will turn gray/red)

5. **Tap "Save"**

**Result**: Product is now hidden from customers but still in your list. You can turn it back ON anytime.

**When to hide products:**
- Out of stock for a long time
- Product not available seasonally
- Waiting for new stock to arrive

---

## Deleting a Product

### To permanently remove a product:

1. **Open the product** (tap on it)

2. **Tap "Delete" button** (trash icon)

3. **Confirm deletion** when asked

**‚ö†Ô∏è Warning**: Deleted products cannot be recovered. If you might stock the product again later, **hide it instead** (see above).

## Managing Stock Levels

### Daily Stock Updates:

**When stock is low:**
1. Edit the product
2. Update the **Stock** number
3. Save

**When stock is OUT:**
- Option 1: Set Stock to `0` and hide the product
- Option 2: Just hide the product (turn Visible OFF)

**When stock arrives:**
- Edit product
- Update Stock number
- Turn Visible ON
- Save

## Common Questions

### Q: How long does it take for changes to appear in the customer app?

**A**: Usually 30-60 seconds. Customers might need to refresh their browser or close/reopen the app to see updates immediately.

---

### Q: Can I add the same product twice with different prices?

**A**: Yes, but use different Product IDs. Example:
- `prod020` - Tomatoes (grade A) - ‚Çπ50/kg
- `prod021` - Tomatoes (grade B) - ‚Çπ40/kg

---

### Q: What if I make a mistake while adding a product?

**A**: Just edit the product immediately after creating it, or delete it and create a new one.

---

### Q: Can I add products from my computer?

**A**: No, the Glide app is mobile-only. You can also edit the Google Sheet directly from a computer if needed (ask your technical assistant).

---

### Q: How many products can I add?

**A**: No limit! Add as many products as you need.

---

### Q: Can I add multiple photos for one product?

**A**: The app supports one photo per product. Choose the best photo that shows the product clearly.
